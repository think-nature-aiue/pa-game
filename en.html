🌿 Nature Reserve Design Game

Survey and protect a 30-cell field using a limited budget of 30 points. Your final score is the sum of (protected cells × species points) across all species.

Rules
· Each species occupies a contiguous range of cells — no gaps.
· Rarer species (smaller range) are harder to find but worth more points per protected cell.
  Species A·B·C (range 10) = 1 pt  /  Species D·E·F (range 5) = 2 pt  /  Rare G·H (range 2) = 5 pt
· Rare species only appear where ≥3 other species co-occur. A first survey shows "?" — a re-survey (2 pt) is needed to confirm presence.
